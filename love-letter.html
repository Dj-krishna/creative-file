I Love You
Exceptionally

beyond measure  ·  beyond words  ·  beyond time

In the spectrum of every color the sky has ever shown, you are the hue my heart has always called its home. Warm as the amber of a quiet sunrise glow, alive as the green where tender wildflowers grow.
Deep as the ocean beneath an open sky of blue, vast as the indigo stretched between the stars — that is the love I carry, whole and true, a love no distance, no season, no world can mar.
You are the reason every dawn arrives with grace, the quiet warmth beneath each ordinary day. In you I find the color every rainbow chased — a beauty more than any word could ever say.
So here, with every color that the universe can lend, with every hue the light of love can name — I paint these words across the sky, and tend this heart that burns for you, and will not dim or tame.

forever & always, yours

Your Loving Husband  ♡
